The Problem

India's First Line of Healthcare Defense is Flying Blind

For 80% of Indians, the neighbourhood chemist is their first — and often only — point of clinical contact. Yet they operate with zero intelligence infrastructure.

💊

Dead Stock Crisis

Independent chemists carry 30–40% more inventory than needed due to poor demand forecasting, tying up working capital in medicines that expire unsold.

₹40,000+ lost per chemist per month
⚠️

Unsafe Dispensing

With no clinical decision support, dangerous drug-drug interactions are missed. A tired pharmacist serving 100+ patients/day cannot catch every contraindication from memory.

~3M adverse drug events/year in India
📱

The Digital Gap

While e-pharmacy giants and hospital chains deploy AI and data science teams, independent chemists are still managing prescriptions on paper and billing on basic POS systems.

95% have no AI/analytics tools
🏥

No Tele-Consult Bridge

Walk-in patients with unclear symptoms get guessed-at OTC recommendations. There is no structured pathway to connect them to a doctor from the pharmacy floor.

60% of pharmacy visits lack a valid Rx
The Solution

SetuAI 3.0 — The Accountable Chemist Platform

A federated intelligence layer — privacy-first, SLM-powered, ABDM-compliant — that transforms the independent pharmacy into India's most trusted primary care access point.

🧠

Clinical Decision Support

Real-time drug interaction flags, dosage alerts, and symptom-to-condition inference at the point of dispensing. The chemist becomes a safe co-prescriber, not a guesser.

📦

Predictive Inventory AI

Local disease pattern analysis + seasonal demand modelling auto-triggers restocking before stockouts occur. Reduces dead stock by 25% within 6 weeks of activation.

🩺

Tele-Consult Bridge

60-second specialist connection from the pharmacy counter. Patients get prescriptions, chemists close sales, doctors reach tier-2/3 patients — everyone wins.

Outcome Accountability Layer

Pharmacist Equity Pool

Network effects that benefit chemists, not just the platform. Participating pharmacies earn equity-like tokens from aggregate anonymised insights sold to pharma manufacturers.

How It Works

From Setup to Intelligence in 48 Hours

1

Onboard in Minutes

Chemist downloads the SetuAI app, links their existing billing system via API or manual import, and completes a 20-minute guided setup. No hardware required.

2

AI Learns Your Inventory Pattern

Within 48 hours, the local SLM builds a demand-forecasting baseline from 90 days of transaction history, cross-referenced with city-level disease surveillance data.

3

Clinical Guardrails Activate at Billing

Every prescription scanned at billing is checked against drug interaction databases in real-time. Dangerous combinations trigger a mandatory review step — before the patient pays.

4

Tele-Consult for Undiagnosed Patients

For walk-ins without a prescription, the chemist triggers a tele-consult flow. Doctor connects within 60 seconds. Prescription issued digitally, dispensed immediately.

5

Outcomes Tracked, Equity Accumulated

Patient adherence data (anonymised) feeds the Pharmacist Equity Pool. Chemists with better outcome scores earn revenue share from aggregated insights sold to pharma partners.
